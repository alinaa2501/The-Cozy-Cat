Again, no one to leave your cat with during your vacation?

Now you don’t need to ask someone to stay with your cat or reschedule your trip to other dates, because you can entrust your pet to our cat hotel The Cozy Cat

What we offer

Our mission is simple: at a minimum, to provide your pet with the most comfortable conditions during your departure, and, at a maximum, to turn his stay in our hotel into a cat vacation to which he will want to return again and again.

Comfortable conditions

A 100 m² room with play, rest, dining, and restroom areas, plus access to a fenced garden. Limited spots available!

Premium food

Premium food tailored to your cat’s needs, with a bonus fish/meat dish twice a week. Owner approval required for specific ingredients.

Good care

Cats of various breeds, with two staff always present. Certificates for health and vaccinations are required before check-in.
